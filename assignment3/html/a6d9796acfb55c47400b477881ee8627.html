The JV Boys Basketball team had its 11-game winning streak broken at Frederick Douglass tonight as the JV Cards lost by a score of 61-40. The JV Cards fell to 15-4 on the season.

The JV Cards travel to Henry Clay on Thursday, February 15th for a 6PM tip-off. This will be the final JV contest of the season.

Go Cards!
